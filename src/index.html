About Us

At Iron Wing Dispatching, we specialize in providing personalized dispatch services tailored to the unique needs of truck owner-operators and small motor carriers. Our mission is to maximize your profitability by finding the best loads, negotiating with brokers and shippers, and handling the logistics — so you can focus on what you do best: driving.

Led by Nick Kalas, a former trucker with first hand experience, Iron Wing Dispatching understands the challenges of the road and the value of a reliable dispatcher. We’re here to enhance your trucking experience and help you grow your business with professionalism and care.
