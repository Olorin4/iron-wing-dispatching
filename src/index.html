About Us

At Iron Wing Dispatching, we specialize in providing personalized dispatch services tailored to the unique needs of owner-operators and small motor carriers. Our mission is to maximize your profitability by finding the best loads, negotiating with brokers, and handling the logistics—so you can focus on driving.

What sets us apart is our firsthand experience. Before launching Iron Wing Dispatching, our founder spent years behind the wheel as an owner-operator. He knows the long hours on the road, the unpredictability of freight markets, and the frustration of dealing with endless paperwork. That’s why we’re committed to going beyond the basics. We understand the pressures you face because we’ve felt them ourselves. We use that insight to deliver solutions that not only streamline operations but also anticipate your challenges—from securing reliable loads that fit your schedule to managing compliance so you’re never caught off guard.

At Iron Wing, you’re not just another carrier—you’re a partner. We’re here to enhance your trucking experience, support your growth, and help you succeed in an industry we know inside and out.
